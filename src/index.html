About Us

Fruit Havana provides a variety of fruit juice drinks such as smoothies, blended juices, crushed juices, and Granita Tea ice blended at the Pasar Malam located in Kampar, Perak. Besides that, we develop our traditional way of business into an online drinks delivery platform, allowing the customer to have online orders without physical restriction. However, we also remain the existing business in Pasar Malam, the customers can choose to order online or physically. We provide consumers with an online platform on purchasing our various fruit juice drinks and the information about our drinks and our stall.
